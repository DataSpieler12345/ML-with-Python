German Credit Risk Classification

data = https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kabure/german-credit-data-with-risk

Dataset Information

The original dataset contains 1000 entries with 20 categorial/symbolic attributes prepared by Prof. 
Hofmann. In this dataset, each entry represents a person who takes a credit by a bank. 
Each person is classified as good or bad credit risks according to the set of attributes.
